Your Questions, Answered

Is this couples therapy?

No. Think of it as Strava for your relationship. It’s a private tool to track data and spot trends. While it's not a replacement for therapy, it can be a powerful complement to self-awareness.

Does my partner have to use it?

Nope. It's designed for solo reflection first. You gain clarity for yourself, which is the foundation for any change. Inviting a partner is an optional feature we're planning for v2.

Will this make me obsessive about tracking?

We're designing against addiction. No daily streaks, no guilt-trips if you skip days. This is a tool you pull out when you need clarity, not another app demanding your daily attention.

How is my data kept private?

Your privacy is fundamental. When we launch, all entries will be end-to-end encrypted, and our AI analysis is anonymized. We are building this on the principle that your reflections belong only to you.
